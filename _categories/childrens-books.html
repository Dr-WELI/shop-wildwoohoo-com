What this catalogue will hold

Sets and booklets

  • Slipcase set. Four field-guide booklets in a slipcase, the way the line is meant to be read. Around AU$70 per set. The first is A Kangaroo Mob Across Seasons.
  • Single booklets. Each booklet also sells on its own, 8 to 16 pages, around AU$18.
  • Companion. A web companion with real animal recordings, links to open-access video and science, and maps. Free with the book, unlocked by a code printed inside the back cover. Not a subscription, and it collects nothing about the child. An optional parent and teacher pack adds lesson plans and a cross-series guide for the grown-ups.

Every booklet is bilingual, English and Portuguese, cited to named research, and ends by sending the reader outside. Each carries the AU Consumer Law guarantee, an age recommendation, and care language. EU paperbacks ship through print-on-demand partners that act as the EU Responsible Person inside their warehouse model.
