First product

Books companion

  • Bilingual read-aloud by Dr WELI in EN and PT.
  • Field-recorded animal sounds tied to the species in the story.
  • IUCN distribution maps showing where the animal narrators live.
  • Fact decks with one science point per page, sourced.
  • Drawing prompts for kids to extend the story.
  • Parent guides with conversation prompts and at-home extensions.
  • Open-access citation chain so teachers and parents can follow the science.

Sold as a family subscription at AU$8.50/month or AU$72/year, unlocked through a printed code inside the back cover of book one. Future books extend the same companion rather than launching parallel apps.
