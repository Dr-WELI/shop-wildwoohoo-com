First product

Books companion

Every booklet carries a code inside the back cover. An adult enters it once to open that booklet's companion. The child-facing core is free with the book, it is not a subscription, and it collects nothing about the child.

  • Bilingual read-aloud of the booklet by Dr WELI, in EN and PT.
  • Field-recorded animal sounds for the species in the booklet.
  • Distribution maps from open data, showing where each animal lives.
  • Fact cards with one science point each, sourced.
  • Printable researcher notebook pages for observation and drawing.
  • The booklet's sources linked, so teachers and parents can follow the science.

For the grown-ups

Parent and teacher pack

An optional pack, sold separately, that spans the whole series. It is bought and used by an adult. It never asks the child for an account and never tracks the reader.

  • Curriculum-alignment notes for AU primary.
  • Lesson plans and classroom extension activities.
  • A parent guide with conversation prompts across the books.
  • The full cross-series citation chain in one place.

The child-facing companion stays free and data-free. The paid pack is an adult resource and never appears inside the child's companion. Pricing to confirm.
