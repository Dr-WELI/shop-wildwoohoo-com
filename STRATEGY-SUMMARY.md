## The platform picture

The strategy doc proposes a **two-surface split**:

- **shop.wildwoohoo.com** (this repo, GitHub Pages now, intended for Shopify Basic later) holds: children's books (ebook + paperback + companion subscription), POD merch, limited drops, digital downloads (kits + stems + course materials), companion-app subscriptions.
- **welimusic.bandcamp.com** (recommended handle) holds: WELI music releases, music-tied merch.

The two surfaces share footer cross-links but never duplicate catalogues. Music stays in the music ecosystem; everything else stays in the brand ecosystem.

The strategy doc originally specified `wildwoohoo.com.au` as the Shopify storefront. WELI's task brief (2026-06-08) reframes the shop as `shop.wildwoohoo.com`, a subdomain of the existing main site. The main site already links `<a href="https://shop.wildwoohoo.com">Shop</a>` in its nav, so the subdomain framing is the live convention. The `.com.au` framing is preserved in the strategy doc; if WELI wants to re-align, that's a future decision (see `TODO.md`).

## Pricing convention

- **Round AU$** figures only. AU$45 not AU$44.99. Round pricing signals B-Corp craft positioning per shop immersion Insight 8.
- **Title format:** `WildWooHoo | [Product Name] | [Variant]` per Insight 4. Max 70 chars for Google product card.
- **Meta description:** 150-160 chars, hand-written, never auto-generated per Insight 5.

## Compliance posture

- **GST.** Recommendation: voluntary registration before shop opens, deadline 2026-07-31 (per FIN_SP). Avoids retroactive registration risk, gives GST claimability on Printful + Shopify + Stripe fees.
- **AU Consumer Law.** Inline guarantee language on every product page. 30-day returns. Faulty = brand pays return; change-of-mind = customer pays.
- **EU GPSR (in force since 13 December 2024).** Every EU-shipped product needs a Responsible Person + technical file + warnings in destination language. Posture for v1: **POD-only EU shipping** (Printful EU acts as Responsible Person inside its warehouse model). Inventory-held items (limited drops, vinyl) ship AU/NZ only until a third-party EU RP service engages.
- **Children's products.** Small-parts warning, age recommendation, wash-before-use language on every kids' product page.
- **ICIP discipline.** Zero product copy or design invokes Indigenous Australian art / motif / story / symbology without documented consultation, royalty agreement, and attribution chain. Acknowledgement of Country at brand level (footer, about page) only; not on individual product pages.
- **Greenwashing.** Every sustainability claim carries inline evidence (supplier, certification, percentage). No claim without citation.

## The 35 shop-immersion insights (one-line digest)

These are condensed from `~/Studio/Industry-Knowledge/immersion/shop/insights.md`. Apply them when writing real product copy.

1. **First sentence.** Name the thing, locate it. Don't open with "Introducing", a question, or the founder story.
2. **Materials honesty.** Name supplier / textile spec / certification. Specificity is the brand.
3. **Acknowledged limits.** Three-beat: claim, claim, limitation we're addressing. Trust signal in 2026.
4. **Shopify title format.** `WildWooHoo | [Product] | [Variant]`. Max 70 chars.
5. **Meta description.** 150-160 chars, hand-written. Pattern: type + specificity + soft commitment.
6. **Seven images.** Hero, scale, detail, lifestyle, alternate, packaging, model. Order matters.
7. **Sustainability claim-evidence pairing.** Inline citation, not separate manifesto.
8. **Round pricing.** AU$45 not AU$44.99. Charm pricing signals discount; round signals premium.
9. **Shipping honesty.** Be transparent about international costs. No "free shipping worldwide" fine print.
10. **EU GPSR.** POD-only EU for v1.
11. **AU Consumer Law.** Inline guarantee language.
12. **What this is not.** 2-3 sentences pre-empting misconception.
13. **Brand-specific lifestyle imagery.** Named location and person, not generic.
14. **Limited editions.** Exact count + public reason + no quiet re-release.
15. **Photo background consistency.** Pick one (white / brand-tone / scene) and stay there.
16. **Closing brand sentence.** Pull to the brand without restating the slogan.
17. **Voice of the creator.** "We" is studio; "I" is WELI on artist-line products only.
18. **Music-merch artefact framing.** CDs / vinyl ship with zines + signed inserts + extras.
19. **POD disclosure.** Name POD inline. Customers tolerate POD when named; punish when discovered.
20. **Returns.** 30-day window, faulty = brand pays, change-of-mind = customer pays.
21. **compare_at_price.** Only for documented discounts, never theatrical scarcity.
22. **Inventory vs POD copy register.** Inventory can be sensory ("the cotton feels..."); POD must stay to spec.
23. **Children's products.** Age, supervision, wash-before-use, EU GPSR translations.
24. **Scannable copy.** Bolded micro-headings + short paragraphs + 4-5 bullet feature list.
25. **Specific care instructions.** Tied to textile spec + sustainability rationale where data supports.
26. **Permission-based opt-ins.** Specific transactional, not generic newsletter, on product pages.
27. **AUD + GST.** Domestic prices GST-inclusive. Don't hard-code prices in body copy.
28. **Bundle pricing.** Coherent story, not discount theatre.
29. **Reviews.** Don't seed fake. Build organically.
30. **International shipping honesty.** Transparent rates, no dark-pattern fine print.
31. **Indigenous-art-inspired products.** Out of scope without documented consultation. ICIP discipline.
32. **Brazilian / Portuguese inclusion.** Natural to WELI's identity, not exoticised. PT appears where it would in WELI's regular speech.
33. **No stock photography.** Original or commissioned only. Stock destroys credibility.
34. **Copy length.** Tees / posters / stickers: 80-120 words. Zines / CDs / vinyl / books: 200-300 words.
35. **Cross-sell as brand statement.** Curate "you may also like" manually; teach the brand range.

## What does NOT belong on the shop

- **Music releases.** Bandcamp (planned: welimusic.bandcamp.com).
- **Sync licensing / EPK.** wildwoohoo.com/music-and-entertainment/sync/ (M&E surface, not shop).
- **Workshop bookings, residencies, sponsor packages.** Bespoke, routed through cockpit and direct.
- **Academic / programme work.** drweli.com surface.
- **Internal apps** (editor, papers, brain, etc.). Not consumer-ready. Books-companion is the only consumer-app surface in v1.
