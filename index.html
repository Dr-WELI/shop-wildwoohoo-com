05 / Questions

Common questions

What does the WildWooHoo Shop sell?

Children's books, classroom kits and educational materials, prints, wear and digital tools from WildWooHoo Studio. It is science-art work made for families and educators.

When does the shop open?

The first catalogue, the Kangaroo Time classroom kit and stems, opens in Q3 2026. The Kangaroo Time children's books follow in Q1 2027.

Who is behind WildWooHoo Shop?

WildWooHoo Studio, founded by Dr WELI (Dr Weliton Menário Costa), a behavioural ecologist (PhD, ANU) and AAAS Dance Your PhD Global Winner 2024.

Are the materials suitable for Australian classrooms?

Yes. The educational kits are classroom-ready for AU primary and secondary, bilingual English and Portuguese where the source workshop was.

Where does the shop ship from?

Sydney, Australia, with Australia and New Zealand served first.

How do I find out when it opens?

Register your interest on this page and we will send one email when the shop opens.
