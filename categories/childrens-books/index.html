Formats and price bands

  • Ebook. AU$12-18. First title ships Q1 2027.
  • Trade paperback. AU$25-30. Print-on-demand via IngramSpark and KDP Print. First title ships Q2 2027.
  • Companion app. A web app unlocked by a code printed in the back of each physical book. Bilingual read-aloud by Dr WELI, field-recorded animal sounds, distribution maps, fact decks, drawing prompts, parent guides, open-access citation chain. AU$8.50/month or AU$72/year per family.
  • Series. Book two follows in Q3 2027, book three in 2028.

Every physical book carries the AU Consumer Law guarantee inline, plus age recommendation and care language. EU GPSR posture is print-on-demand only for v1, so EU paperbacks ship through Printful or IngramSpark partners that act as the EU Responsible Person inside their warehouse model.
