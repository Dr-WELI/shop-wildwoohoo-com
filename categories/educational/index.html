What this catalogue will hold

Kits, packs and stems

  • Workshop kits. PDF + worksheets + audio + video, packaged from the studio's delivered programmes. AU$15-35 per kit. Kangaroo Time classroom kit opens the catalogue in Q3 2026.
  • Methodology kits. Step-by-step playbooks for studio methods, such as the Dance Your PhD kit (Global Winner 2024 evidence) at AU$35.
  • Logic Pro stems and project files. Kangaroo Time and Cabra da Peste stem packs, plus the kangaroo-vocal-stack session template. AU$25-45 per pack. For educational use, with sync-supervisor preview alongside.
  • Slide template packs. Sci-comm slide decks in the brand fonts. AU$15.
  • Lead-sheets and chord charts. Vocal and piano sheets for the Why We Smile songs as they release. AU$8-12.
  • Course bundles. Kit + slides + stems + lead-sheet for a coherent classroom unit. AU$120.

Digital downloads are the fastest-shipping catalogue and the one the shop opens with. The first product appears in Q3 2026.
